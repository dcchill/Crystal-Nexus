# Getting Started and Progression

Crystal Nexus is a tech and energy mod built around crystals, machines, FE transfer, ore processing, and big late-game builds. Early on, you will work with End Crystals and Ancient Crystal materials. Later, the mod opens up into Blutonium, Invertium, Polymer, EE-Matter, singularities, reactors, and Zero Point power.

## Early Goals

### Crystal Extractor

The Crystal Extractor is the first major utility item for the crystal progression path.

- Use: Right-click an End Crystal to collect its energy crystal.
- Sneak use: Crouch + right-click can pick the extractor back up.
- Special case: Dragon Energy Crystals are obtained from End Crystals summoned by the Ender Dragon.

The extractor leads into unstable and stable crystal crafting, which powers the main FE generation chain.

### Energy Crystal Tiers

Energy crystals are used in machines and power systems. When used in a Crystal Energy Siphon, they generate different amounts of FE.

### Crystal Purifier

The Crystal Purifier upgrades and stabilizes crystal materials, and is also involved in creating Crystalized Alloy.

The basic rule of thumb is 2 x Crystals will get you the next tier of crystal, except for the blutonium crystal.

### Crystal Energy Siphon

- Place an energy crystal in the slot.
- Place End Crystals in range.
- The block gathers FE from nearby End Crystals.
- It outputs energy to the block directly below.
- Base End Crystal search reach is about 3 blocks from the Siphon.
- Generation is per End Crystal in range.

## First Machine Chain

A practical early machine setup is:

1. Energy Generator for FE generation.
2. Battery Cell or another energy-capable block for buffering.
3. Basic Energy Cable, Energy Cable, or beam transfer to move FE.
4. Crystal Ore Crusher for raw ore to dust.
5. Crystal Dust Separator for dust to nuggets.
6. Crystal Smelter or Iron Smelter for smelting.
7. Conveyor Belts for basic automation.
